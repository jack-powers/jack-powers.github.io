**Last updated: March 9, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to all Referee Fitness Test mobile applications ("Apps") developed by Jack Powers ("we," "us," or "our").

### Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.** Our Apps do not require user accounts, do not track your location, and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data entered into the Apps (such as fitness test results) is stored locally on your device only and is not accessible to us or any third party.

### Data Storage

Any data you enter into the Apps is stored solely on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted.

### Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

### Children's Privacy

Our Apps do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
